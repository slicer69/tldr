opusenc

Convert WAV or FLAC audio to Opus.

More information: home page

- Convert WAV to Opus using default options:
opusenc path/to/input.wav path/to/outputhome pageopus
- Convert stereo audio at the highest quality level:
opusenc --bitrate 512 path/to/input.wav path/to/outputhome pageopus
- Convert 5.1 surround sound audio at the highest quality level:
opusenc --bitrate 1536 path/to/input.flac path/to/outputhome pageopus
- Convert speech audio at the lowest quality level:
opusenc path/to/input.wav --downmix-mono --bitrate 6 path/to/outhome pageopus
