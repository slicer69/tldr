shutdown


Description: Shutdown and reboot the system.

- Power off (halt) immediately:
shutdown -h now
- Reboot immediately:
shutdown -r now
- Reboot in 5 minutes:
shutdown -r +{{5}} &
- Shutdown at 1:00 pm (Uses 24h clock):
shutdown -h 13:00
- Cancel a pending shutdown/reboot operation:
shutdown -c
