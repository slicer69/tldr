vlc

Cross-platform multimedia player.

More information: home page

- Play a file:
vlc path/to/file
- Play in fullscreen:
vlc --fullscreen path/to/file
- Play muted:
vlc --no-audio path/to/file
- Play repeatedly:
vlc --loop path/to/file
- Play video from a URL:
vlc https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oHg5SJYRHA0
